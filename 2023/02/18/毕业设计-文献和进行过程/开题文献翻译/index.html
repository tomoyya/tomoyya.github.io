一种带有集成信号磁耦合器的新型同步无线电力和信息传输系统

发表于 2023-02-18 更新于 2023-02-19 分类于 毕业设计 ， 文献

IEEE论文A New Simultaneous Wireless Power and Information Transfer System With Integrated Signal Magnetic Coupler 是我本科毕业设计开题时要求的翻译文献

Abstract

For a reliable and controllable wireless power transfer (WPT) system, the simultaneous wireless power and information transfer (SWPIT) technology has recently attracted special consideration to achieve real-time information exchange between the transmitter and receiver. In this article, a novel SWPIT system with an integrated signal magnetic coupler is proposed for wireless charging applications. The signal detection coil integrated with the receiver coil is designed to utilize the existing ferrite cores of the magnetic coupler, reducing the overall cost and obtaining a more compact design. Meanwhile, the deviation frequency enlargement effect (DFEE) principle and the frequency shift keying (FSK) technology are used to realize SWPIT in a wireless charger. The equivalent circuit of the proposed system is modeled and analyzed considering the crosscouplings between the integrated signal coil and the main coils.
为了实现可靠和可控的无线电力传输（WPT）系统，同步无线电力和信息传输（SWPIT）技术最近引起了特别的关注，以实现发射器和接收器之间的实时信息交换。在这篇文章中，提出了一种新型的带有集成信号磁耦合器的SWPIT系统用于无线充电应用。与接收线圈集成的信号检测线圈的设计利用了磁耦合器现有的铁氧体磁芯，降低了整体成本，实现了更紧凑的设计。同时，利用偏差频率放大效应（DFEE）原理和频移键控（FSK）技术来实现无线充电器的SWPIT。考虑到集成信号线圈和主线圈之间的交叉耦合，对拟议系统的等效电路进行了建模和分析。

The finite element analysis (FEA) is presented through Ansys Maxwell software to select the optimal ferrite core structure and eliminate these unwanted cross-couplings. Finally, a 70-W power experimental prototype is established, and comprehensive experimental results are given to verify the feasibility and effectiveness of the proposed SWPIT system. Furthermore, the experimental results demonstrate that the measured output power fluctuation is below 0.5 % during information transmission, the transmission rate is up to 9.6 kbps, and the system efficiency can be maintained at 88.76 %. Index Terms—Integrated magnetic coupler, wireless power transfer (WPT), information transfer, constant output voltage.
通过Ansys Maxwell软件进行了有限元分析（FEA），以选择最佳的铁氧体磁芯结构并消除这些不需要的交叉耦合。最后，建立了一个70 W功率的实验原型，并给出了综合实验结果，以验证所提出的SWPIT系统的可行性和有效性。此外，实验结果表明，在信息传输过程中，测量的输出功率波动低于0.5%，传输速率可达9.6 kbps，系统效率可保持在88.76%。

Index Terms—Integrated magnetic coupler, wireless power transfer (WPT), information transfer, constant output voltage.
关键词—集成磁耦合器，无线电力传输（WPT），信息传输，恒定输出电压。

I. INTRODUCTION

DUE to the advantages of convenience, non-contact, safety and reliability, the wireless charging system has attracted special consideration [1]–[5]. Based on the different working principles, wireless charging technology mainly includes inductive wireless charging, capacitive wireless charging, and microwave wireless charging. Wireless charging also can be referred to as wireless power transfer (WPT). In recent years, the inductive power transfer (IPT) has been widely used in powering household appliances [1], smartphones [3], electric vehicles [4], [6], [7] and biomedical implants [8]. For most applications, reliable information exchange between the transmitter and receiver is usually required in closed-loop control to track the maximum efficiency, adjust the output transfer power or monitor the load status [4], [6], [9], [10].
由于无线充电系统具有方便、非接触、安全和可靠等优点，它已经引起了人们的特别关注[1]-[5]。基于不同的工作原理，无线充电技术主要包括感应式无线充电、电容式无线充电和微波无线充电。无线充电也可以被称为无线电力传输（WPT）。近年来，感应式电力传输（IPT）已被广泛用于为家用电器[1]、智能手机[3]、电动汽车[4]、[6]、[7]和生物医学植入物[8]供电。对于 大多数应用来说，在闭环控制中，通常需要在传输器和接收器之间进行可靠的信息交换以跟踪最大效率，调整输出转换功率或监测负载状态[4] ，[6]，[9]，[10]。

Generally, traditional communication technologies such as Bluetooth, 2.4G module, Wi-Fi and Zigbee are widely adopted in the WPT applications for wireless data transfer [3], [9]. However, these technologies need complicated address matching and are easily affected by the electromagnetic noise caused by the high-frequency switch [9], [11]. Additionally, additional communications devices may increase the system complexity and consume higher system costs. Therefore, the research on simultaneous wireless power and information technology (SWPIT) has gained a lot of attention recently [11]–[21].
一般来说，传统的通信技术，如蓝牙、2.4G模块、Wi- Fi和Zigbee（紫蜂）在WPT应用中被广泛采用，用于无线数据传输[ 3]，[9]。然而，这些技术需要复杂的地址匹配，并且容易受到高频开关引起的电磁噪声的影响[9], [11]。此外，额外的通信设备可能会增加系统的复杂性， 消耗更高的系统成本。因此，关于同步无线供电和通信技术（SWPIT）的研究最近得到了很多关注[11]-[21]。

According to different implementations, there are three methods to implement SWPIT: adding one or two extra coils severing as the communication antennas, injecting highfrequency signal information into the power channel, and combining information trannel with the power channel based on the shared modulation. One uses two pairs of orthogonal coils to transfer information and power independently [12]. An extra signal coil is designed at the pick-up side to realize information transmission based on the third-order harmonic of the triangular current waveform [13]. However, the crosscoupling between the auxiliary coil and main coils would result in a low signal-to-noise ratio [14]. In [15], a magnetically decoupled coupling structure is proposed to alleviate the cross interference between the two pairs of coils. Nevertheless, in order to realize information transmission, extra modulation devices would lead to higher costs and increasing volume.
根据不同的实现方式，有三种方法来实现SWPIT：增加一个或两个额外的线圈作为通信天线，将高频信号信息注入电力通道，然后基于共享的调制将信息频道与电力通道相结合。一个使用两对正交线圈来独立传输信息和功率[12]。在接收端设计一个额外的信号线圈以实现基于三角电流波形的三次谐波的信息传输[13]。然而，辅助线圈和主线圈之间的交叉耦合会导致低信噪比[14]。在[15] 中，提出了一种磁去耦耦合结构来缓解两对线圈之间的交叉干扰。然而，为了实现信息传输，额外的调制装置将导致更高的成本和更大的体积。

The second method is injecting weak energy with data signals into the power transmission channel. A shared physical information channel is designed for wireless charging applications in [16]. This scheme allows the data and power to transfer relatively independently without crosstalk interference by adding wave trappers (passive inductors and capacitors). These trappers are used to separate and filter the power and data signal at different frequencies within the IPT system. It is complicated and challenging to configure these wave trappers to operate in an accurate resonant state for a better filtering effect [15]. In order to improve the bit rate and interference immunity of the information transmission, a full-duplex SWPIT using frequency division multiplexing is reported in [18].
第二种方法是将带有数据信号的微弱能量注入到电力传输通道。在[16]中为无线充电应用设计了一种共享的物理信息通道。该方案通过添加捕波器（无源电感和电容）， 使数据和电力的传输相对独立而没有串扰干扰。这些捕波器用于分离和过滤IPT系统内不同频率的电力和数据信号。将这些捕波器配置成在准确的谐振状态下运行以达到更好的过滤效果是非常复杂和具有挑战性的[15]。

In order to improve the bit rate and interference immunity of the information transmission, a full-duplex SWPIT using frequency division multiplexing is reported in [18]. The data carrier is injected and extracted by a plug-and-play toroidal-core inductor to realize high-speed simultaneous communication. In [19], [20], the power and information are transmitted at different frequencies to transmit the forward and backward data. This method requires another carrier device to generate the data carrier frequency, which is usually much higher than the power transmission trannel [21]. Therefore, it would increase the complexity of these systems. Additionally, the power consumption of signal modulators and demodulators can not be ignored, especially in low-power systems.
为了提高信息传输的比特率和抗干扰性，[18]中报道了一种使用频分复用的全双工SWPIT。数据载体由一个即插即用的环形磁芯电感注入和提取，实现高速同步通信。在[19]、[20]中，电力和信息以不同的频率传输，以传输前向和后向数据。这种方法需要另一个载波设备来产生数据载波频率，这通常比电力传输频道高得多[21]。因此，这将增加这些系统的复杂性。此外，信号调制器和解调器的功耗也不容忽视，特别是在低功率系统中。

The last method utilizes the power waveform as the carrier and modulator. The compensation network and the power generator are shared with the information transfer channel, which makes this method require the minimum hardware cost. The frequency shift keying (FSK) [13], [22], amplitude shift keying (ASK) [23] and phase shift keying (PSK) [19], [24] are widely used modulation methods in information modulation. Based on multiplexing mode in a quasi-resonant flyback converter, the data transmission between primary and secondary sides is utilized by adjusting the duty ratio and switching frequency of the primary side inverter [25].However, the coupling coefficient between the main coils should be fixed in this information transfer approach. Simultaneously, this method may inevitably lead to systematic output voltage and current fluctuation [19], [21].
最后一种方法是利用电力波形作为载波和调制器。补偿网络和电源发生器都与信息传输通道共享，这使得这种方法需要最小的硬件成本。频移键控（FSK）[13], [22],幅移键控（ASK）[23]和相移键控（PSK）[19], [24] 是信息调制中广泛使用的调制方法。基于准谐振反激式变频器的复用模式，通过调整一次侧变频器的占空比和开关频率来利用一次侧和二次侧之间的数据传输[25]。然而，在这种信息传输方法中，主线圈之间的耦合系数应该是固定的。同时，这种方法可能不可避免地导致系统输出电压和电流的波动[19],[21]。

Based on the selected harmonic elimination pulse width modulation, a forward simultaneous wireless information and power transfer method is proposed in [26], and an added chopper circuit is connected to the rectifier to achieve constant voltage output. To combat the above problems, the transmitted information is detected and recovered based on the harmonic components in the resonant circuit [27]. However, the large volume of the detection coil may limit its practical applications.
基于选定的谐波消除脉宽调制，[26]中提出了一种前向同步无线信息和功率传输方法，并在整流器上连接了一个附加的斩波电路以实现恒定的电压输出。为了解决上述问题，根据谐振电路中的谐波成分来检测和恢复传输的信息[27]。然而，检测线圈的大体积可能限制其实际应用。

In our article, by the above analysis of the SWPIT system, the signal coil is designed to integrate with the main coils for a compact system. The integration of the resonant inductor into the magnetic coupler has been proposed to save space and ferrite cores of the wireless charger in [28], [29]. As reported in [28], the cost of each wireless charger may be saved by $50.99 with the design of an integrated resonant inductor. And the fully integrated magnetic structure is proposed in [30], where the dc-dc inductor and the resonant inductor are both integrated into the main coils. It demonstrates that the integrated system reduces the estimated cost by around 35 % and 291 cm3 per wireless charging system.
在我们的文章中，通过对SWPIT系统的上述分析，信号线圈被设计成与主线圈集成在一起，以获得一个紧凑的系统。在[28],[29]中已经提出了将谐振电感集成到磁耦合器中，以节省无线充电器的空间和铁氧体芯。如[28]中报告的那样，每个无线充电器的成本可以通过集成谐振电感的设计节省50.99美元。而完全集成的磁性结构是在[30]中提出的，其中直流电感和谐振电感器都集成在主线圈中。它表明，该集成系统使每个无线充电系统降低了约35%的预估成本和291立方厘米的体积。

Although integrating the resonant inductor and the dc-dc inductor into the magnetic coupler has been studied recently, the integration of the signal coil of the magnetic coupler has not been analyzed. Integrating the signal coil into the magnetic coupler leads to new design challenges, such as the unwanted interaction between the signal coil and the magnetic coupler, especially under misalignment conditions. The unwanted interaction would damage the signal transmission and reduce the power transfer efficiency. Then, the integrated coil should be designed and optimized to decouple from the magnetic coupler.
尽管最近已经研究了将谐振电感和直流电感集成到磁耦合器中，但还没有分析磁耦合器集成信号线圈的问题。将信号线圈集成到磁耦合器中会导致新的设计挑战，如信号线圈和磁耦合器之间有害的相互作用，特别是在错位条件下。有害的相互作用会破坏信号传输并降低电力传输效率。因此，应设计和优化集成线圈，使其与磁耦合器去耦。

This article proposes a novel SWPIT system with an integrated magnetic coupler design to help save implementation cost and size and shrink output fluctuation during information transmission. In this paper, the power and information are simultaneously wireless transferred based on the deviation frequency enlargement effect (DFEE) principle. That is to say, the fundamental component is employed to transfer power and the third-harmonic component is used to transmit information.The signal modulation is implemented by binary FSK (2FSK) technology, and the signal coil integrated with the receiver side coil is designed to separate the fundamental and third-harmonic components.
本文提出了一种新型的SWPIT系统，该系统采用一体化的磁耦合器设计，来帮助节省实施成本和尺寸，并缩小信息传输过程中的输出波动。在本文中，基于偏差频率放大效应（DFEE）原理，电力和信息同时进行无线传输。也就是说，用基频分量来传输功率，三次谐波分量来传输信息。信号调制由二进制FSK（ 2FSK）技术实现，信号线圈与接收端线圈集成设计，以分离基波和三次谐波成分。

Considering the cross-couplings between the integrated signal coil and the main coils, the equivalent circuit model of this proposed system is established and analyzed. The proposed magnetic coupler is simulated through finite element analysis (FEA) to select the optimal ferrite core structure and eliminate these unwanted crosscouplings. Finally, a 70-W power experimental prototype is built to verify the feasibility and effectiveness of the proposed SWPIT system.
考虑到集成信号线圈和主线圈之间的交叉耦合，我们建立并分析了该提议系统的等效电路模型。通过有限元分析（FEA）对所提出的磁耦合器进行了模拟，以选择最佳的铁氧体磁芯结构并消除这些有害的交叉耦合。最后，建立了一个70W功率的实验原型，来验证所提出的SWPIT系统的可行性和有效性。

The rest of this paper is organized as follows: firstly, the system structure is given in Section II, and it also demonstrates the working principle and theoretical analysis of the proposed system. Then, the proposed integrated magnetic coupler and the FEA are studied in detail in Section III. Later in Section IV, the correctness and feasibility of the proposed system are verified by the experimental results. Finally, the conclusion is drawn in Section V.
本文其余部分的组织结构如下：首先，在第二节中给出了系统的结构，同时也展示了所提系统的工作原理和理论分析。然后，在第三节中详细研究了所提出的集成磁耦合器和有限元分析。之后在第四节中，通过实验结果验证了所提系统的正确性和可行性。最后，在第五节中得出结论。

II. SYSTEM STRUCTURE AND THEORETICAL ANALYSIS

A. System Structure

The schematic circuit of the proposed SWPIT system with the integrated signal coil is depicted in Fig. 1. The system consists of a transmitting module, a modulation circuit, a secondary picking module and a demodulation circuit. The transmitting module is constituted by a full-bridge inverter (S1 - S4), a dc power supply Uin, an input filter capacitor Cin and a transmitting compensation network (a transmitting coil L1 and a resonant capacitor C1). The secondary picking module is constituted of a power receiving module and a signal detecting module. The former is composed of a picking coil L2, a resonant capacitor C2, a full-bridge rectifier (D1 - D4), an output filter capacitor Co and a load RL. The last module comprises a signal coil Ln and a resonant capacitor Cn.
图1描述了所提出的带有集成信号线圈的SWPIT系统的电路原理图。该系统由一个发射模块、一个调制电路、一个二次选通模块和一个解调电路组成。发射模块由一个全桥逆变器（S1 - S4 ）、一个直流电源Uin 、一个输入滤波电容Cin 和一个发射补偿网络（一个发射线圈L1 和一个谐振电容C1）构成。二次选通模块由电源接收模块和信号检测模块构成。前者由一个拾取线圈L2、一个谐振电容C2、一个全桥整流器（D1 - D4）、一个输出滤波电容Co和一个负载RL 组成。最后一个模块由一个信号线圈Ln和一个谐振电容Cn 组成。

R1, R2 and Rn are the internal resistors of L1, L2 and Ln, respectively. Vp and Vs are the input and output voltages of the compensation topology, and Ip and Is are the input and output currents of the compensation topology. Vn represents the voltage of the signal coil, which is detected and used to demodulate the transmit information through a demodulation circuit.
R1 、R2 和Rn分别为L1 、L2和Ln的内部电阻。Vp和Vs是补偿拓扑结构的输入和输出电压，而Ip和Is是补偿拓扑结构的输入和输出电流。Vn代表信号线圈的电压，它被检测并用于通过解调电路对发射信息进行解调。

To achieve highly efficient wireless power transmission, the transmitting and receiving compensation should satisfy
为了实现高效的无线功率传输，发射和接收补偿应该满足
$$
f = 1/2\pi\sqrt{L_1C_1} = 1/2\pi\sqrt{L_2C_2} \tag{1}
$$
where $f$ is the switching frequency, and $\omega=2\pi f$.

The full-bridge inverter is applied to the converter dc source into an assigned-frequency square power to drive the transmitting module. The equivalent ac load resistor Req and the voltage of the inverter Vp can be expressed as [4], [6]
全桥逆变器被应用于将直流电源转化为指定频率的方形功率来驱动发射模块。等效交流负载电阻 $R_{eq}$ 和逆变器的电压 $V_{p}$ 可以表示为[4], [6]
$$
R_{eq} = \frac{8}{\pi^2}R_L = \frac{8}{\pi^2}\frac{V_{Load}}{I_{Load}},V_p = \frac{2\sqrt{2}}{\pi}U_{in} \tag{2}
$$
where $V_{Load}$ and $I_{Load}$ are the load voltage and current, respectively.

As depicted in Fig. 1, the information modulation shares the same modulator of the power modulation. The transmission data is loaded onto the inverter by pulse width modulation (PWM) through 2FSK modulation. The mixed PWM signal is employed to drive the inverter. Then, power and data can be transferred to the secondary side wirelessly by the contactless charging system. By separating the fundamental and the third harmonic components of the wave in the receiver coil, the information demodulation is completed through the signal detecting module. The parameter design of the signal detecting circuit is given as follows
如图1所示，信息调制与功率调制的调制器相同。传输数据通过脉冲宽度调制（PWM）经2FSK调制加载到变频器上。混合的PWM信号用来驱动变频器。然后，电力和数据可以被通过非接触的充电系统将其无线传输到二次侧。通过在接收线圈中分离波的基波和三次谐波成分，信号检测模块就完成了信息的解调。信号检测电路的参数设计给定如下
$$
3f = \frac{1}{2\pi\sqrt{L_nC_n}} \tag{3}
$$
In order to reduce the volume of the receiver on the vehicle side and save the design cost. This paper proposes a new magnetic structure to integrate the signal coil with the picking coil on the receiver side. As shown in Fig. 2, the double-D (DD) structure is selected as the main coils for the high misalignment tolerance ability and the magnetic coupler structure is composed of ferrite bars and DD coils. The dimensions of the magnetic coupler and the transfer air gap are also presented in Fig. 2. The detailed design process will be demonstrated in the next section.
为了减少飞行器侧接收器的体积以及节省设计成本。本文提出一种新的磁性结构，将信号线圈与接收端的拾取线圈整合在一起。如图2所示，为了提高错位容忍能力我们选择双D（DD）结构作为主线圈，磁耦合器结构由铁氧体棒和DD 线圈组成。该磁性耦合器和传输气隙的尺寸也在图2中列出。详细的设计过程将将在下一节演示。

Integrating the signal coil with the main coils will result in unwanted couplings between the signal coil and the main coils. The signal coil should be decoupled from the main coils to eliminate the unwanted mutual inductors. Due to the natural decoupling characteristic of two DD coils placed in an orthogonal manner, the signal coil is designed as a DD layout and placed orthogonal to the receiving coil. The dimensions of the signal coil are the same as that of the main coils, as illustrated in Fig. 2. The mutual coupling coefficients among the transmitter and receiver coils (k12), the signal and transmitter coils (k1n), and the signal and receiver coils (k2n) are defined as
将信号线圈与主线圈整合在一起将导致它们之间有害的耦合。信号线圈应与主线圈去耦以消除不必要的互感。由于以正交方式放置的两个DD线圈的自然去耦特性，信号线圈被设计成DD布局，并与接收线圈正交放置。信号线圈的尺寸和主线圈是相同的，如图2所示。发射器和接收器线圈(k12)、信号和发射器线圈(k1n )以及信号和接收器线圈(k2n)之间的相互耦合系数定义为
$$
k_{12} = M_{12} / \sqrt{L_1 L_2} \
k_{1n} = M_{1n} / \sqrt{L_1 L_n} \
k_{2n} = M_{2n} / \sqrt{L_2 L_n} \tag{4}
$$
where the $M_{ij}$ are the mutual inductors between $L_i$ and $L_j$ (i = 1, 2; j = 2, n). The design and optimization of the integrated magnetic structure are presented in Section III.

B. Model and Analysis of the Proposed System

The equivalent circuit of the proposed SWPIT system is shown in Fig. 3, and $I_n$ represents the current flowing through the signal detecting module. $\dot{X}{ik} (X = V, i = p;X = I, i = 1, 2, 3, n)$ are the RMS values of $k$th component of the corresponding voltage or current. According to the Kirchhoff’s voltage law (KVL), the current of the proposed system can be derived from $\dot{I}k = Z^{-1} \dot{V}k$ ,where $\dot{V}k = \begin{bmatrix} \dot{U}{pk} & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}^T$ and $\dot{I}k = \begin{bmatrix} \dot{I}{1k} & \dot{I}{2k} & \dot{I}{3k} \end{bmatrix}^T$ .Then, the differential equations of this system can be described as
拟议的SWPIT系统的等效电路如图三所示，$I_n$ 代表流经信号检测模块的电流。 $\dot{X}{ik} (X = V, i = p;X = I, i = 1, 2, 3, n) $ 是相关的电压或电流的第k分量的有效值。根据基尔霍夫电压定律（KVL），所提出系统的电流可以由 $\dot{I}k = Z^{-1} \dot{V}k$ 导出，其中 $\dot{V}k = \begin{bmatrix} \dot{U}{pk} & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}^T$ 而 $\dot{I}k = \begin{bmatrix} \dot{I}{1k} & \dot{I}{2k} & \dot{I}{3k} \end{bmatrix}^T$ 那么，该系统的微分方程可以描述为
$$
\dot{U}{pk} = (R_1 + jk\omega L_1 + \frac{1}{jk\omega C_1} ) \dot{I{1k}} - jk\omega M_{12}\dot{I}{2k} + jk\omega M{1n} (\dot{I}{2k} - \dot{I}{3k}) \tag{5}
$$

$$
\begin{align*}

& 0 = (jk\omega L_2 + R_2 + Z_{nk}) \dot{I}{2k} + jk\omega M{1n} \dot{I}{1k} + jk\omega M{2n} \dot{I}{2k} \
& - jk\omega M{2n}(\dot{I}{2k} - \dot{I}{3k}) + jk\omega M_{12}\dot{I}{1k} - Z{nk} \dot{I}_{3k} \tag{6}

\end{align*}
$$

$$
0 = (Z_{nk} + \frac{1}{jk\omega C_2} + R_{eq}) \dot{I}{3k} - Z{nk} \dot{I}{2k} - jk\omega M{1n} \dot{I}{1k} - jk\omega M{2n} \dot{I}_{2k} \tag{7}
$$

where $Z_{nk}$ represents the equivalent impedance(阻抗) of the signal detecting module and it can be defined as
其中 $Z_{nk}$ 代表信号检测模块的等效阻抗，它可以定义为
$$
Z_{nk} = R_n + jk\omega L_n + \frac{1}{jk\omega C_n} \tag{8}
$$
Therefore, the impedance matrix Z is given as
因此，阻抗矩阵Z给定为
$$
Z = \begin{bmatrix} Z_{11} & Z_{12} & Z_{13} \
-Z_{12} & Z_{22} & Z_{23} \
-Z_{13} &-Z_{23} & Z_{33} \tag{9}
\end{bmatrix}
$$
The diagonal elements of the impedance matrix Z are defined as follows
阻抗矩阵Z的对角线元素定义如下
$$
\begin {align}
& Z_{11} = R_1 + jk\omega L_1 + \frac{1}{jk\omega C_1} \
& Z_{22} = jk\omega L_2 + R_2 + Z_n \
& Z_{33} = Z_{nk} + \frac{1}{jk\omega C_2} + R_{eq} \tag{10}

\end {align}
$$
The nondiagonal elements can be expressed as
非对角线元素可以表示为
$$
\begin{align}

& Z_{12} = -jk\omega M_{12} -jk\omega M_{1n} \
& Z_{13} = -jk\omega M_{1n} \
& Z_{23} = -Z_{nk} + jk\omega M_{2n} \tag{11}
\end{align}
$$
By solving the circuit based on the impedance matrix, we can get each mesh current as follows
通过根据阻抗矩阵求解电路，我们可以得到每个网孔电流，如下所示
$$
\dot{I}{1k} = \frac{Z{22} Z_{33} + Z^2_{23}} {\Delta} \dot{U}_{pk} \tag{12}
$$

$$
\dot{I}{2k} = \frac{Z{12} Z_{33} - Z_{13} Z_{23}} {\Delta} \dot{U}_{pk} \tag{13}
$$

$$
\dot{I}{3k} = \frac{Z{12} Z_{23} + Z_{13} Z_{22}} {\Delta} \dot{U}_{pk} \tag{14}
$$

where
$$
\Delta = Z_{11}Z_{22}Z_{33} + Z_{11}Z^2_{23} + Z_{22}Z^2_{13} + Z_{33}Z^2_{12} .
$$
