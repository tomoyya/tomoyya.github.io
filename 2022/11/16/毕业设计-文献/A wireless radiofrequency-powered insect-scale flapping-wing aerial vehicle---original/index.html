A wireless radiofrequency-powered insect-scale flapping-wing aerial vehicle

发表于 2022-11-16 更新于 2022-11-24 分类于 毕业设计 ， 文献

nature论文A wireless radiofrequency-powered insect-scale flapping-wing aerial vehicle

Abstract

Insect-scale aerial vehicles are useful tools for communication, environmental sensing and surveying confined spaces. However, the lack of lightweight high-power-density batteries has limited the untethered flight durations of these micro aerial vehicles. Wireless power transmission using radiofrequency electromagnetic waves could potentially offer transmissivity through obstacles, wave-targeting/focusing capabilities and non-mechanical steering of the vehicles via phased-array antennas.
昆虫规模的飞行器是用于通信、环境感应和勘测密闭空间的有用工具。然而，由于缺乏轻量级的高功率密度电池，限制了这些微型飞行器的无拘束飞行时间。使用射频电磁波的无线电力传输有可能提供穿越障碍物的传输能力、波的瞄准/聚焦能力以及通过相控阵天线对飞行器进行非机械性的转向。

But the use of radiofrequency power transmission has so far been limited to larger vehicles. Here we show that a wireless radiofrequency power supply can be used to drive an insect-scale flapping-wing aerial vehicle. We use a sub-gram radiofrequency power receiver with a power-to-weight density of 4,900 W kg–1, which is five times higher than that of off-the-shelf lithium polymer batteries of similar mass. With this system, we demonstrate the untethered take off of the flapping-wing micro aerial vehicle. Our RF-powered aircraft has a mass of 1.8 g and is more than 25 times lighter than previous radiofrequency-powered micro aerial vehicles.
但到目前为止，射频功率传输的使用仅限于更大的航具。在这里，我们表明，一个无线射频电源可以用来驱动一个昆虫规模的拍打翼飞行器。我们使用一个亚克级射频功率接收器，其功率重量密度为4,900 W kg-1，比类似质量的现成的锂聚合物电池高五倍。利用这个系统，我们展示了拍打翼微型飞行器的无系留起飞。我们的射频动力飞行器的质量为1.8克，比以前的射频动力微型飞行器轻25倍以上。

Main

Micro aerial vehicles (MAVs) are of potential use in environmental sensing1, communication2,3, indoor entertainment4, search and rescue5, and the autonomous inspection of confined or dangerous areas6. However, MAV regulations are being tightened due to the risk of accidents from crashing MAVs. In the USA, for example, drones weighing over 250 g must be registered, and drone flight areas are heavily restricted7. Japan has also recently announced that the threshold weight of MAVs subject to flight restrictions will be reduced from 200 to 100 g (ref. 8). These intensified requirements demand smaller and lighter MAVs. In particular, as the collision energy of a falling aerial vehicle strongly depends on the vehicle mass, the safety of MAVs can be increased by designing and developing lightweight insect-scale MAVs.
微型飞行器（MAVs）在环境传感1、通信2,3、室内娱乐4、搜索和救援5以及对封闭或危险环境进行自主检查方面6具有潜在的用途。然而，由于飞行器坠毁的事故风险，飞行器法规正在收紧。例如，在美国，重量超过250克的飞行器必须进行登记，而且无人机飞行区域受到严格限制7。日本最近也宣布，受飞行限制的无人机的阈值重量将从200克降至100克（参考文献8）。这些加强的要求需要更小更轻的微型飞行器。特别是，由于坠落的飞行器的碰撞能量在很大程度上取决于飞行器的质量，因此可以通过设计和开发轻量型昆虫级的飞行器来提高微型飞行器的安全性。

The critical components in developing a fully untethered insect-scale MAV are the actuator, drive electronics and power supply. Although larger-scale MAVs generally employ electromagnetic motors as actuators, their efficiency is notably reduced when the vehicle size is decreased due to friction and resistance losses9. There are several other actuation mechanisms, including electrostatic, shape memory alloy and piezoelectric mechanisms. Considering the high speed and high output required for flights, piezoelectric actuation is the most promising mechanism10 and is commonly employed. As piezoelectric actuation generates reciprocal motion, a bioinspired flapping-wing mechanism is more suitable for insect-scale MAVs than a rotating propeller11,12,13,14,15. Lightweight, high-voltage and high-power electronics for piezoelectric actuation have also been studied16,17,18. However, the low energy density of conventional batteries makes them unsuitable for insect-scale MAVs19.
开发完全无系留的昆虫级MAV的关键部件是致动器、驱动电子设备和电源。尽管较大规模的MAVs通常采用电-磁电机作为致动器，但由于摩擦和阻力损失，当飞行器尺寸减小时，其效率明显降低9。还有其他几种驱动机制，包括静电、形状记忆合金和压电机制。考虑到飞行所需的高速度和高输出，压电致动是最有前途的机制10并被普遍采用。由于压电致动产生往复运动，仿生的拍翼机制比旋转螺旋桨更适合昆虫规模的MAVs11,12,13,14,15。用于压电驱动的轻量级、高电压和高功率电子设备也被研究过16,17,18。然而，现有电池的低能量密度使其不适合于昆虫规模的MAVs19。

The most successful work of a battery-powered MAV is the DelFly20,21. DelFly Nimble is, for example, a bird-scale 29 g tailless vehicle capable of hovering and moving in all directions21. DelFly Micro is an insect-scale model with a mass of 3 g (ref. 20). However, this vehicle can only fly forward with the help of lift during translational movement and has no hovering capability. A similar vehicle (3.2 g, battery powered) has been developed with a maximum thrust force (4 gram-force (gf)) that exceeds its mass, but it lacks the additional actuators necessary for controlled flight22. At the insect scale, there are currently no battery-powered vehicles capable of both controlled flight and hovering by supplying enough lift to support their own weight. Thus, alternative energy sources with higher power density are required to realize insect-scale MAVs that can fly freely.
电池供电的MAV最成功的作品是DelFly20,21。例如，DelFly Nimble是一个鸟类规模的29克的无尾飞行器，它能够悬停并向各个方向移动21。DelFly Micro是一个昆虫规模的模型，质量为3克（参考文献20）。然而，这种飞行器只能在横移过程中借助于升力向前飞行，而且不具备悬停能力。已经开发了一个类似的飞行器（3.2克，电池供电），其最大推力（4克力（gf））超过了它的质量，但它缺乏控制飞行所需的附加驱动器22。在昆虫规模上，目前还没有电池供电的飞行器能够通过提供足够的升力来支持自身的重量，从而实现控制飞行和悬停。因此，需要有更高功率密度的替代能源，以实现能够自由飞行的昆虫规模MAVs。

The flight time of a bird-scale battery-powered MAV is usually less than 10 min. Therefore, even if an insect-scale battery-powered MAV can be created, it would be expected to have a short flight duration. To overcome this drawback, wireless power transmission via lasers or light has been developed23,24. However, for indoor applications or in confined spaces, laser/light power transmission for small MAVs has serious limitations. Laser/light power transmission is easily blocked by obstacles, and fast and robust laser steering technology (for example, phased-array lasers25,26) is still not readily available off the shelf. In contrast, radiofrequency (RF) electromagnetic waves can penetrate insulating obstacles or objects smaller than their wavelength, as demonstrated by mobile phone communications. These characteristics provide considerable advantages for indoor applications. In addition, beam-steering technology implemented by a phased-array system is very practical27,28,29. Previous research on MAVs with RF power transmission has been limited to large multirotor vehicles weighing over a few dozen grams30,31,32,33,34, and the feasibility of RF power transmission for insect-scale MAVs is still uncharted.
鸟类规模的电池供电的MAV的飞行时间通常少于10分钟。因此，即使可以创造一个昆虫规模的电池供电的MAV，预计它的飞行时间也会很短。为了克服这个缺点，已经开发了通过激光或光的无线电力传输23,24。然而，对于室内应用或密闭空间，小型MAVs的激光/光能传输有严重的局限性。激光/光能传输很容易被障碍物阻挡，而且快速和强大的激光转向技术（例如相控阵激光器25,26）仍然不是现成的。相比之下，射频（RF）电波可以穿透绝缘障碍物或小于其波长的物体，正如移动电话通信所证明的那样。这些特点为室内应用提供了相当大的优势。此外，由相控阵系统实现的波束转向技术也非常实用27-29。以前对射频功率传输的MAVs的研究仅限于重量超过几十克的大型多旋翼飞行器30-34，而射频功率传输对于昆虫规模的MAVs的可行性仍是未知数。

Beyond MAVs, there are earlier reports on RF power transmission to insect-scale robots35,36. In particular, a walking robot powered by a piezoelectric actuator with a mass of 2.1 g has been developed35. The power-receiving circuit in this robot has a maximum power output of 1 W and a mass of 200 mg, which may be applicable for the flight of an MAV of equivalent weight. However, the power transmission technology used in these studies is based on near-field electromagnetic waves; the robot cannot be far away from the transmitter. To transmit power to MAVs that can fly freely, a system using radiative, far-field electromagnetic waves is desirable.
除了MAVs之外，还有更早的关于射频功率传输到昆虫级机器人的报告35,36。特别是，一个由质量为2.1克的压电执行器驱动的行走机器人已经被开发出来35。该机器人的功率接收电路的最大功率输出为1瓦，质量为200毫克，这可能适用于同等重量的MAV的飞行。然而，这些研究中使用的功率传输技术是基于近场电磁波的；机器人不能远离发射器。为了向可以自由飞行的MAVs传输功率，最好是使用辐射式的远场电磁波的系统。

In this Article, we report a sub-gram RF power receiver for 5 GHz far-field radiative electromagnetic waves with a power density that is sufficient for flight. We install the electronics on an insect-scale flapping-wing vehicle (Fig. 1a); the piezoelectric flapping-wing actuators are on the upper half of the vehicle and the electronics are mounted on the lower half. The electronics consist of three sets of 5 GHz dipole power-receiving antennas/rectifiers, a high-voltage three-channel driver for the flapping-wing actuators and a microprocessor. The vehicle has six independently actuated wings that are actuated in pairs (Fig. 1b). For a configuration with the same direction of motion in actuators and wings, this paired actuation scheme results in a low vibration energy loss and high lift force efficiency37,38. Level flight can be achieved by adjusting the lift forces of the three pairs of actuator, and the vehicle can be successfully launched by the RF power supply with a transmission power of 30 W (Fig. 1c and Supplementary Video 1). Our vehicle is more than 25 times lighter than the previous vehicles powered by RF (Extended Data Table 1).
在这篇文章中，我们报告了一个亚克级的射频功率接收器，用于5GHz的远场辐射电磁波，其功率密度足以用于飞行。我们将电子装置安装在一个昆虫规模的拍打翼飞行器上（图1a）；压电的拍打翼驱动器在飞行器的上半部，电子装置安装在下半部。电子装置包括三套5GHz偶极子功率接收天线/整流器，一个用于拍打翼执行器的高压三通道驱动器和一个微处理器。该飞行器有六个独立驱动的机翼，它们成对驱动（图1b）。对于致动器和机翼运动方向相同的配置，这种成对的致动方案导致了低振动能量损失和高升力效率37,38。通过调整三对致动器的升力可以实现水平飞行，并且通过传输功率为30W的射频电源可以成功发射飞行器（图1c和补充视频1）。我们的飞行器比以前由射频供电的飞行器要轻25倍以上（扩展数据表1）。

Power-receiving electronic system

Photographs and system schematic of the developed high-power-density, lightweight electronics are shown in Fig. 2a,b, respectively. The circuit was fabricated using standard mass-production flexible printed circuit (FPC) technology. Thermal management is a key factor for achieving a high power density because any concentrated overheating results in high power loss as well as destruction of the microscale electrical components. To distribute the power-loss heat, a multi-antenna/rectifier configuration was employed; the 5 GHz electromagnetic wave is received by three dipole antennas and converted to direct current (d.c.) power by three rectifiers (the schematic is shown in Extended Data Fig. 1a). The dipole antennas are fabricated from the FPC wiring of Cu patterns, such that the mass of the antenna is only 4 mg. The maximum power-receiving efficiency of the rectifier was approximately 67% (Extended Data Fig. 1c).
图2a、b分别显示了所开发的高功率密度的轻质电子器件的照片和系统原理图。该电路是使用标准的大规模生产的柔性印刷电路（FPC）技术制作的。热管理是实现高功率密度的一个关键因素，因为任何集中的过热都会导致高功率损失以及微尺度电气元件的破坏。为了分散功率损失的热量，采用了多天线/整流器配置；5GHz电磁波由三个偶极子天线接收，并由三个整流器转换为直流电（d.c.）功率（示意图见扩展数据图1a）。偶极子天线是由铜制的FPC布线制作的，因此，天线的质量只有4毫克。整流器的最大功率接收效率约为67%（扩展数据图1c）。

Because the voltage of the received electrical power can exceed the maximum input voltage of the digital components, that is, the microprocessor, it should be regulated to a digital-level voltage. We separated part of the electrical power and adjusted it to 3.3 V using a voltage regulator for the microprocessor (CC2640R2F, Texas Instruments). The remaining electrical power was also regulated with an upper limit of 6.0 V, which is the maximum input voltage of the 3.3 V voltage regulator. Next, the power source was boosted to yield a constant high voltage (175 V maximum) using a d.c.–d.c. converter (the schematic is shown in Extended Data Fig. 1b), as required for the piezoelectric drive. Then, it was reshaped into three square-wave actuation signals by a three-channel high-voltage driver controlled by the microprocessor (schematic of the microprocessor and high-voltage driver are shown in Extended Data Fig. 2a,b, respectively; the driving waveform is illustrated in Extended Data Fig. 3; connection diagram of the subcircuits is shown in Extended Data Fig. 2c).
由于接收到的电力电压可能超过数字元件，即微处理器的最大输入电压，因此应将其调节到数字电压水平。我们分离了部分电力，并使用微处理器的电压调节器（CC2640R2F，德州仪器）将其调节到3.3V。剩余的电力也被调节到6.0V的上限，这是3.3V电压调节器的最大输入电压。接下来，使用一个直流-直流转换器（原理图见扩展数据图1b）对电源进行升压，以产生一个恒定的高电压（最大175V），这是压电驱动所需要的。然后，通过一个由微处理器控制的三通道高压驱动器将其重塑为三个方波驱动信号（微处理器和高压驱动器的原理图分别见扩展数据图2a、b；驱动波形见扩展数据图3；子电路的连接图见扩展数据图2c）。

Note that the high output voltage is restricted by the limit of transistors used in the high-voltage driver (T3 and T4; Extended Data Fig. 2a) in our system. The average power consumed by the microprocessor is only 20 mW, and most of the power is supplied to the actuation system. The square-wave actuation signal was 115 Hz, which was matched with the resonant frequency of the flapping-wing actuators to maximize the wing stroke. Finally, the square waves are sent to the flapping-wing actuators. The d.c.–d.c. converter efficiency was greater than 80% over a wide range of output power with a maximum efficiency of 83.1%, as shown in Fig. 2c.
请注意，高输出电压受到我们系统中高压驱动器（T3和T4；扩展数据图2a）中使用的晶体管的限制。微处理器所消耗的平均功率只有20毫瓦，大部分的功率都提供给了致动系统。方波致动信号为115赫兹，与拍打翼致动器的谐振频率相匹配，以使机翼行程最大化。最后，方波被送到拍打翼执行器上。如图2c所示，在广泛的输出功率范围内，直流-直流转换器的效率大于80%，最大效率为83.1%。

Figure 2d shows the output voltage for the continuous driving of the electronics; the electronics successfully produced a high-voltage actuation signal without thermal runaway. The maximum output power of the power-receiving circuit (antenna and rectifiers) is 1.2 W, which is designed to meet the required power at the d.c.–d.c. converter start up. The total weight of the power receiver was 245 mg, and the power density was 4,900 W kg–1. This is much higher than off-the-shelf lithium polymer batteries, for example, the mass and power density of a GM300910 battery (PowerStream Technology) are 330 mg and 897 W kg–1, respectively, and those of GEB201212C (PowerStream Technology) are 500 mg and 1,200 W kg–1, respectively.
图2d显示了电子装置连续驱动的输出电压；电子装置成功地产生了高压驱动信号而没有热失控。电源接收电路（天线和整流器）的最大输出功率为1.2 W，这是为了满足直流-直流转换器启动时的所需功率。功率接收器的总重量为245毫克，功率密度为4,900 W kg-1。这比现成的锂聚合物电池要高得多，例如，GM300910电池（PowerStream Technology）的质量和功率密度分别为330毫克和897 W kg-1，而GEB201212C（PowerStream Technology）的质量和功率密度分别为500毫克和1200 W kg-1。

Flapping-wing actuator and its performance

The actuation mechanism is another key performance factor for MAVs. The developed vehicle employs a very simple ‘direct-drive’ mechanism for wing actuation, as shown in Fig. 3a. The 32.40-mm-long biomimetic wing is directly connected to a 16.50-mm-long piezoelectric bending actuator composed of a 0.10-mm-thick piezoelectric layer laminated to a 0.13-mm-thick titanium elastic plate. The bending actuator was designed as a trapezoid with the top and bottom sides measuring 4.4 and 8.5 mm, respectively, to avoid stress concentration. Owing to the high piezoelectric coefficient of single-crystalline Pb(In1/2Nb1/2)O3–Pb(Mg1/3Nb2/3)O3–PbTiO3 (TRS Technologies)39 used in this study (its effective piezoelectric coefficient d32 is −1,156 pm V–1 (ref. 39), which is two times higher than that of typical commercial piezoelectric ceramics under large electric fields40), large flapping motions are possible without the need for a complex transmission mechanism to amplify the motion if the strain during actuation is sufficiently smaller than the fracture strain of the piezoelectric material.
驱动机制是MAVs的另一个关键性能因素。所开发的飞行器采用了非常简单的 “直接驱动 “机制来驱动机翼，如图3a所示。32.40毫米长的仿生机翼直接连接到一个16.50毫米长的压电弯曲致动器，该致动器由0.10毫米厚的压电层和0.13毫米厚的钛弹性板层压而成。弯曲激励器被设计成梯形，其顶部和底部的边长分别为4.4和8.5毫米，以避免应力集中。由于本研究中使用的单晶Pb(In1/2 Nb1/2 )O3 -Pb(Mg1/3 Nb2/3 )O3 -PbTiO3 (TRS Technologies)39 的高压电系数（其有效压电系数d32 是 -1,156 pm V-1（参考文献。39），比典型的商业压电陶瓷在大电场下的系数高2倍40），如果驱动过程中的应变足够小于压电材料的断裂应变，就可以实现大的拍打运动，而不需要复杂的传输机制来放大运动。

In our previous design15, we achieved 312 mgf of lift (sinusoidal drive with a peak-to-peak voltage of 100 V) per actuator with a mass of 252 mg. To obtain sufficient payload capacity even with the installed electronics, we have improved the lift by reducing the vibration energy loss of the paired-wing configuration, and we have reduced the mass without reducing the lift by downsizing the actuators; this redesign was implemented in a previous report38, and the approach of the redesign is described in Supplementary Section 4. The lift force and power consumption can be controlled by the duty ratio18,41,42.
在我们以前的设计中15，我们实现了每个驱动器312mgf的升力（正弦波驱动，峰-峰电压为100V），质量为252mg。为了在安装了电子装置的情况下也能获得足够的有效负载能力，我们通过减少配对翼配置的振动能量损失来提高升力，并通过缩小致动器的尺寸来减少质量而不降低升力；这种重新设计在以前的报告中已经实现38，重新设计的方法在补充章节4中描述。升力和功率消耗可以通过占空比来控制18,41,42。

Figure 3b,c shows the performance of the fabricated paired actuator with a square-wave driving signal having a voltage amplitude of 140 V and frequency of 115 Hz. Here the driving signal was generated by a combination of high-voltage driver circuit and d.c. voltage source (PMX350-0.2A, Kikusui Electronics). Thus, the consumed power includes the loss in the high-voltage driver, which is expected to be less than 1 mW (mainly due to the leakage current of the n-type metal–oxide–semiconductor field-effect transistor (nMOSFET); T3 and T4 in Extended Data Fig. 2a), which is much smaller than the handling energy. The average maximum lift force per paired actuator is 880 mgf, allowing the MAV to take off; 600 mgf per paired actuator is required to launch the MAV because its total weight is 1,810 mg. From Fig. 3d, the required power per paired actuator is approximately 130 mW, and the total power consumed by the actuators is estimated to be 390 mW. The efficiency of the d.c.–d.c. converter at an output power of 390 mW required for continuous flight was 83% (Fig. 2c). Considering this efficiency in addition to the power consumed by the microprocessor and voltage regulator (20–36 mW), the total power consumption for continuous flight is approximately 500 mW. The lift-to-power efficiency including the electronics (namely, d.c.–d.c. converter, high-voltage driver and microprocessor) was 3.6–3.7 gf W–1. This value is higher than that reported for previous insect-scale flapping-wing MAVs (FWMAVs); for example, 2.4–3.0 gf W–1 was reported in an earlier study24. Considering the efficiency of the RF receiver, a received electromagnetic-wave power of approximately 500 mW/67% ≅ 750 mW was required for continuous flight (the power flow diagram for continuous flight is shown in Extended Data Fig. 4).
图3b,c显示了在电压振幅为140V、频率为115Hz的方波驱动信号下，制造的成对驱动器的性能。这里的驱动信号是由高压驱动电路和直流电压源（PMX350-0.2A，菊水电子）组合产生的。因此，消耗的功率包括高压驱动器的损耗，预计低于1毫瓦（主要是由于n型金属氧化物半导体场效应晶体管（nMOSFET）的泄漏电流导致的；扩展数据图2a中的T3和T4），这比处理能量小很多。每个成对致动器的平均最大升力为880mgf，允许MAV起飞；每个成对致动器需要600mgf来发射MAV，因为其总重量为1810mg。从图3d来看，每个成对驱动器所需的功率大约为130毫瓦，驱动器所消耗的总功率估计为390毫瓦。在连续飞行所需的390毫瓦的输出功率下，直流-直流转换器的效率为83%（图2c）。考虑到这个效率，再加上微处理器和电压调节器的功率（20-36毫瓦），连续飞行的总功率消耗约为500毫瓦。包括电子设备（即直流-直流转换器、高压驱动器和微处理器）在内的升力-功率效率为3.6-3.7gf W-1。这一数值高于以前的昆虫级拍翼无人机（FWMAVs）的报告；例如，在早期的研究中报告的是2.4-3.0 gf W-1 24。考虑到射频接收器的效率，连续飞行所需的接收电磁波功率约为500 mW/67% ≅750 mW（连续飞行的功率流图见扩展数据图4）。

The power consumption of the piezoelectric actuator includes the reactive power due to charging/discharging of the capacitive component. The capacitance C of the paired actuators is approximately 50 nF, and the reactive power is estimated to be CV2f = 113 mW with an ideal square-wave drive at 140 V (peak to peak) and 50% duty ratio. The total power consumption was approximately 230 mW under this condition (measured with a high-voltage amplifier and a function generator for the ideal square-wave drive). Thus, the real power is about 117 mW, and the power efficiency is estimated to be 51%. Because some of the reactive power can be recovered by circuitry18, there is room for future improvement in the efficiency of this system.
压电执行器的功率消耗包括由于电容部件的充电/放电而产生的无功功率。成对驱动器的电容C大约是50 nF，无功功率估计为CV2f = 113 mW，在140 V（峰-峰），占空比50%的理想方波驱动下。在此条件下，总功耗约为230 mW（用高压放大器和理想方波驱动信号的函数发生器测量）。因此，实际功率约为117 mW，功率效率估计为51%。由于一些无功功率可以通过电路18进行恢复，因此该系统的效率在未来还有提高的空间。

The power-to-weight efficiency of the MAV was approximately 400 mW g–1 (including the rectifier efficiency). Therefore, the actuation system of the developed vehicle is as efficient as the metabolic performance of natural bees43,44,45. This overall system efficiency also matches that of small quadcopters (for example, Aerix Quadrotor (10 g, 50 mm span) with an efficiency of ~430 mW g–1).
MAV的功率-重量效率约为400 mW g-1（包括整流器的效率）。因此，所开发的飞行器的驱动系统与天然蜜蜂43-45的新陈代谢性能一样有效。这一总体系统效率也与小型四旋翼飞机（例如，Aerix Quadrotor（10克，50毫米跨度）的效率约为430毫瓦g-1）相匹配。

Untethered flight

The orientation of the power-receiving antennas can be freely configured by folding them like origami. In this experiment, two of the three power-receiving antennas were laid out parallel to each other, whereas the third antenna was laid out orthogonally (Fig. 4a). The orientation of the two antennas coincided with the polarization of the transmitting antenna during standby, and the two antennas received a large amount of power for circuit start up. The orthogonal antenna allows the vehicle to receive energy even when the vehicle is rotating. A horn antenna was chosen for the transmitter; because the electromagnetic waves were transmitted at a constant power (we used 30 W, which is the maximum power of our instruments), the power received by the vehicle depended on its distance from the transmitting antenna. This power characteristic limits the operating distance of the vehicle.
功率接收天线的方向可以通过像折纸一样折叠来自由配置。在这个实验中，三根功率接收天线中的两根是互相平行布置的，而第三根天线是正交布置的（图4a）。两根天线的方向与待机时发射天线的偏振相吻合，而且这两根天线在电路启动时接收了大量的功率。正交的天线允许飞行器在旋转时也能接收到能量。发射器选择了喇叭天线；因为电磁波是以恒定的功率发射的（我们使用30W，这是我们仪器的最大功率），飞行器接收的功率取决于它与发射天线的距离。这种功率特性限制了飞行器的工作距离。

The relationship between the power received at the dipole antenna and the vertical distance of the vehicle from the transmitter is shown in Fig. 4b (three-dimensional distribution of the received power is shown in Extended Data Fig. 5). The vehicle can be operated with a power that is sufficiently large for continuous actuation but not unnecessarily greater than the circuit start-up power to avoid thermal runaway. The operating vertical-distance range was 70–150 mm in the experimental setup. From this result, to maximize the flight distance, we determined the initial distance from the vehicle to the transmitting antenna and transmission power as 70 mm and 30 W, respectively.
图4b显示了偶极子天线接收的功率和飞行器与发射器的垂直距离之间的关系（接收功率的三维分布见扩展数据图5）。飞行器可以在一个足够大以实现连续驱动的功率下运行，但又不会不必要地大于电路的启动功率，以避免热失控。在实验装置中，运行的垂直距离范围是70-150毫米。从这个结果来看，为了最大限度地增加飞行距离，我们确定从飞行器到发射天线的初始距离和发射功率分别为70毫米和30瓦。

The trajectories of three flight trials are shown in Fig. 4c–e, where the zero of the vertical position means the initial position of the vehicles. The flight performance of the vehicle had good reproducibility; the maximum altitudes showed a deviation of 18.8%. We inferred that the trajectory difference was mainly caused by the error in the initial position of the vehicle. Figure 4c shows the accelerations in altitude. These suggest that the flights were not just jumps, but flights with continuous thrust. The maximum altitude in this demonstration was 77.5 mm. The altitude at which the flapping stopped is thought to have been near the inflection point of the trajectory. We calculated the inflection point by fitting the trajectory with a quadratic curve (Extended Data Fig. 6, top). As a result, it was estimated that the actuator stopped at an altitude of 58–66 mm.
三次飞行试验的轨迹见图4c-e，其中垂直位置的零点表示飞行器的初始位置。飞行器的飞行性能具有良好的可重复性；最大高度显示的偏差为18.8%。我们推断，轨迹的差异主要是由飞行器初始位置的误差造成的。图4c显示了高度上的加速度。这些表明，这些飞行不仅仅是跳跃，而是具有持续推力的飞行。这次演示的最大高度是77.5毫米。扑翼停止的高度被认为是在轨迹的拐点附近。我们通过用二次曲线拟合轨迹来计算拐点（扩展数据图6，顶部）。结果，估计驱动器在58-66毫米的高度上停止。

Based on the coordinates at the inflection point and the received power distribution, the received power at the inflection was estimated to be approximately 600 mW (Extended Data Fig. 6, bottom). This coincides with the estimated power required for continuous flight (~750 mW). This is further evidence that the system was continuously powered by the RF supply. The excess lift, excluding the self-weight, during the ascent estimated from the above fitting results was 220–340 mgf. Note that we did not implement closed-loop attitude control in this experiment, because this vehicle was not equipped with any attitude estimation sensor. Instead, we carefully tuned the duty ratios of the actuation signals based on the duty ratio–lift force data (Fig. 3b) to balance the vehicle and achieve level flight; we employed duty ratios of 0.30, 0.47 and 0.49 for the right, left and back actuators, respectively.
根据拐点的坐标和接收功率的分布，拐点处的接收功率被推测为大约600毫瓦（扩展数据图6，底部）。这与估计的连续飞行所需的功率（约750毫瓦）相吻合。这进一步证明了该系统是由射频电源持续供电的。在上升过程中，根据上述拟合结果估计的过剩升力（不包括自重）为220-340mgf。请注意，我们在这个实验中没有实施闭环姿态控制，因为这个飞行器没有配备任何姿态估计传感器。相反，我们根据占空比-升力数据（图3b）仔细调整了驱动信号的占空比，以平衡飞行器并实现平飞；我们对右、左和后部的驱动器分别采用了0.30、0.47和0.49的占空比。

As shown in Fig. 3b, the total lift force is estimated to be about 2.4 gf at the duty ratios setting. On the other hand, the total lift force was estimated to be ~2.0–2.2 gf from the excess lift force calculated from the trajectory described above. We expect that this reduction in lift force is mainly due to the fixed state of the actuator. For the measurement shown in Fig. 3, the paired actuators were rigidly fixed. In the case of the vehicle floating in air, the lift force may have decreased due to the vibration of the entire vehicle. Another possible cause is the tilt of the vehicle. The vehicle was tilted by up to 20° during flight, potentially causing a lift loss of several percentages in the vertical direction.
如图3b所示，在占空比设置下，总升力估计约为2.4gf。另一方面，从上述轨迹描述计算出的多余升力来看，总升力估计约为2.0-2.2 gf。我们预计，这种升力的减少主要是由于驱动器的固定状态造成的。在图3所示的测量中，成对的驱动器是刚性固定的。在飞行器漂浮在空中的情况下，由于整个飞行器的振动，升力可能会下降。另一个可能的原因是飞行器的倾斜。飞行器在飞行过程中最多倾斜了20°，可能导致垂直方向上的升力损失几个百分点。

The most important improvements that need to be made in the present system should be the extension of the operating range (distance) and the reduction in transmission power. To extend the operating range, a system to adjust the power supply according to the distance of the aircraft is needed. The RF power is inversely proportional to the square of the transmitter–receiver distance and proportional to the transmitting power. Thus, if the power is increased, the distance that can be travelled should increase in proportion to the square root of the transmitting power. However, excessive reception energy leads to failure due to overheating of the circuit; therefore, the distance that can be approached by the transmitting antenna also increases. To reduce the transmitting power, a system that focuses on the electromagnetic waves, such as phased arrays, could be promising. Either way, a system that measures the current position of the aircraft and feeds it back to the transmitter will be necessary.
目前系统中需要进行的最重要的改进应该是扩大工作范围（距离）和降低传输功率。为了扩大工作范围，需要一个根据飞机的距离来调整电源的系统。射频功率与发射器-接收器距离的平方成反比，而与发射功率成正比。因此，如果功率增加，可走的距离应与发射功率的平方根成比例增加。然而，过多的接收能量会因电路过热而导致故障；因此，发射天线可达到的距离也会增加。为了降低发射功率，一个专注于电磁波的系统，如相控阵，可能是有希望的。无论哪种方式，一个测量飞机当前位置并将其反馈给发射器的系统将是必要的。

Conclusions

We have shown that RF power receivers can be used as sustainable power sources for insect-scale robots. Our circuit design consists of three dipole antennas/rectifiers to avoid heat concentration, and the high power density was created using a lightweight RF power receiver. We integrated it to an FWMAV38 with a high lift-to-power efficiency. Key performance metrics, namely, power density and total weight of the aircraft, exceed those of existing designs. The power density of our RF power receiver is four times higher than that for off-the-shelf lithium polymer batteries. This is also 2.1 times the power density provided by the solar panel (2,300 W kg–1) used for untethered flight of an insect-scale FWMAV reported previously24. With regard to the total weight, previously reported motor-driven multirotor MAVs with RF power transmission weigh several tens of grams (for example, 51 g (ref. 30) and 85 g); our vehicle is an order of magnitude lighter than these examples.
我们已经表明，射频功率接收器可以作为昆虫规模机器人的可持续电源。我们的电路设计由三个偶极子天线/整流器组成，以避免热量集中，而高功率密度是使用轻型射频功率接收器创造的。我们将其集成到一个具有高升力-功率效率的FWMAV38上。关键性能指标，即功率密度和飞机的总重量，超过了现有的设计。我们的射频功率接收器的功率密度是现成的锂聚合物电池的四倍。这也是以前报道的用于昆虫规模的FWMAV无系留飞行的太阳能电池板（2,300 W kg-1）所提供的功率密度的2.1倍24。关于总重量，以前报道的带有射频功率传输的电机驱动多旋翼飞行器重达几十克（例如，51克（参考文献30）和85克）；我们的飞行器比这些例子要轻一个数量级。

We believe a fully functional untethered MAV with insect-scale mass can be realized by optimizing the vehicle design and adding new features. These include integrating a flight control system in the FWMAV and using a beam-focusing/targeting system for the power transmitter. Beam focusing is also important from a regulatory point of view because the output power of the transmitting antenna is limited below a certain level. For example, according to the Federal Communications Commission (USA) and the Ministry of Internal Affairs and Communications (Japan), the output power of indoor antennas must be less than 1 W (refs. 46,47).
我们相信，通过优化飞行器设计和增加新的功能，可以实现具有昆虫规模质量的全功能无系留飞行器。这包括在FWMAV中集成一个飞行控制系统，并为功率发射器使用一个光束聚焦/瞄准系统。从管理的角度来看，波束聚焦也很重要，因为发射天线的输出功率被限制在一定水平以下。例如，根据联邦通信委员会（美国）和总务省（日本）的规定，室内天线的输出功率必须低于1W（参考文献46,47）。

The use of square waves has the advantage of lower peak-to-peak voltage and a simpler drive circuit compared with those required to generate a sinusoidal actuation signal. On the other hand, the steep drive may increase the risk of actuator damage. The verification of the long-term reliability of this system will be another important future step. Furthermore, a closed-loop flight control system could also be implemented and an altitude-sensing system, such as an inertial measurement unit, could be included in the electronics. An additional antenna for wireless communication is also desirable for practical applications. We note that the developed vehicle has sufficient payload capacity to carry such sensors: the maximum total lift force and payload were 2,620 and 810 mgf, respectively; the typical mass of a commercial inertial measurement unit or communication antenna (such as Bluetooth) is several tens of milligrams only.
使用方波的优点是峰-峰电压较低，与产生正弦驱动信号所需的电路相比，它的驱动电路更简单。另一方面，陡峭的驱动可能会增加驱动器损坏的风险。对该系统的长期可靠性的验证将是未来的另一个重要步骤。此外，还可以实施一个闭环飞行控制系统，并在电子装置中包括一个高度感应系统，如惯性测量单元。对于实际应用来说，一个用于无线通信的额外天线也是可取的。我们注意到，开发的飞行器有足够的有效负载能力来携带这些传感器：最大总升力和有效负载分别为2620和810 mgf；一个商业惯性测量单元或通信天线（如蓝牙）的典型质量仅为几十毫克。

Methods

Vehicle fabrication /飞行器制造

The body and legs of the vehicle were fabricated from glass-fibre-reinforced plastic (GFRP) plates with a thickness of 0.38 mm (FS-10, ACP Composites) and GFRP rods with a diameter of 0.5 mm (Featherfield), respectively. The GFRP parts, flapping-wing actuators and circuit board were joined using an ultraviolet-curable adhesive (LOCTITE 4310). Enamel wire with a diameter of 70 μm was used to wire the actuator to the circuit; the circuit-side and actuator-side ends were connected using a solder and conductive adhesive (CW2400, Chemtronics), respectively. In our earlier reports, we have comprehensively described the fabrication processes for the vehicle (without electronics)38 and the wings48. The mass of the six wings, six actuators, body and three legs before assembly was 107, 696, 340 and 71 mg, respectively. The weight of the assembled vehicle without electronics and wiring was 1,264 mg; 50 mg was added by the adhesive. The image of the vehicle assembly is shown in Supplementary Section 2.
飞行器身和飞行器腿分别由厚度为0.38毫米的玻璃纤维增强塑料（GFRP）板（FS-10，ACP复合材料公司）和直径为0.5毫米的GFRP棒(Featherfield）制作而成。GFRP部件、拍打翼驱动器和电路板使用紫外线固化粘合剂（LOCTITE 4310）进行连接。用直径为70微米的漆包线将驱动器与电路连接起来；电路侧和执行器侧的两端分别使用焊料和导电胶（CW2400，Chemtronics）连接。在我们以前的报告中，我们已经全面描述了飞行器（无电子装置）38和机翼48的制造过程。六个机翼、六个驱动器、飞行器身和装配前的三条腿的重量分别为107、696、340和71毫克。不含电子装置和布线的组装飞行器的重量为1264毫克；50毫克是由粘合剂添加的。飞行器组装的图像显示在补充部分2。

Circuit

The schematic of the power-receiving and converting circuits is shown in Extended Data Fig. 1. The circuit board was manufactured by Cir-Tech (Hamamatsu). The FPC comprises resist lamination, Cu pattern, polyimide, and Cu pattern layers; their thicknesses are 20 µm, 9 µm (with 7 µm Cu platting), 50 µm and 9 µm (with 7 µm Cu platting), respectively. The power-receiving circuit (Extended Data Fig. 1a) consists of rectifiers, voltage-limiting diodes (D2), power storage capacitors (C2) and a voltage regulator (IC1). The voltage-limiting diode protects the d.c.–d.c. converter and limits the output voltage below 6 V (any input beyond this voltage is dissipated as heat loss). The power storage capacitors are used to smooth the output voltage and absorb fluctuations due to the instantaneous load power.
电源接收和转换电路的原理图显示在扩展数据图1中。电路板由Cir-Tech公司（Hamamatsu）制造。FPC由抗蚀剂层、Cu纹、聚酰亚胺和Cu纹层组成；它们的厚度分别为20微米、9微米（含7微米的Cu板）、50微米和9微米（含7微米的铜板）。电源接收电路（扩展数据图1a）由整流器、限压二极管（D2）、功率存储电容器（C2）和一个电压调节器（IC1）组成。限压二极管保护直流-直流转换器，并将输出电压限制在6V以下（任何超过此电压的输入都会以热损耗的形式耗散）。储能电容用于平滑输出电压，并吸收瞬时负荷功率引起的波动。

A voltage regulator is used to supply power to the digital circuits (for example, microcontroller), and the output is 3.3 V. We employed a combination of boost converter and charge pump for the d.c.–d.c. converter; the input voltage (<6 V) is boosted to a maximum of 35 V and then multiplied by five (Extended Data Fig. 1b): 35 × 5 = 175 V. The boost converter is controlled by XC9104D095DR-G (Torex Semiconductor) with an operation frequency of 500 kHz.
我们用一个电压调整器来为数字电路电路（比如微处理器）供电，它的输出为3.3V。我们为直流-直流转换器采用了升压转换器和电荷泵的组合；输入电压（<6 V）被升压到最高35 V，然后乘以5（扩展数据图1b）：35x5=175V。升压转换器由XC9104D095DR-G（Torex Semiconductor）控制，其工作频率为500 kHz。

The high-voltage driving circuit is based on the one developed by us in an earlier study18 (the full schematic is shown in Extended Data Fig. 2a); note that the charge recovery reported in ref. 18 was not used in this experiment. This high-voltage driver is composed of a combination of bipolar transistor and nMOSFET. We selected the components among the available ones along with focusing on withstanding the voltage and mass (the breakdown of the circuit mass is summarized in Extended Data Table 2 and the complete electrical component list is shown in Extended Data Table 3).
高压驱动电路是基于我们在早期研究中开发的电路18（完整的原理图显示在扩展数据图2a）；请注意，参考文献中报告的电荷回收18没有在本实验中使用。这个高压驱动器是由双极晶体管和nMOSFET组合而成。我们在现有的元件中选择了这些元件，同时注重承受电压和质量（电路质量的分解在扩展数据表2中总结，完整的电气元件清单在扩展数据表3中显示）。

The schematic of the rectifier and the simulated performance using electronic design automation software (advanced design system, Keysight Technologies) are shown in Extended Data Fig. 1c–e. There are four types of power entering and leaving the rectifier: input power (PI; Extended Data Fig. 1c), output power (PO; Extended Data Fig. 1c), internal losses (diodes and wires) and reflections back to the input port. Extended Data Fig. 1d shows the internal loss against the input power at a constant PO. When PI increases, the internal loss increases almost proportionally. Since the increase in internal losses leads to the risk of thermal runaway, it is important to appropriately adjust PI. As shown in Extended Data Fig. 1e, the maximum efficiency of the rectifier (PO/PI) was approximately 67% with an optimized input power at PO = 500 mW, which is required for continuous flight. This efficiency is comparable to larger rectifiers49,50.
整流器的原理图和使用电子设计自动化软件（高级设计系统，Keysight Technologies）的模拟性能显示在扩展数据图1c-e。有四种类型的功率进入和离开整流器：输入功率（PI；扩展数据图1c）、输出功率（PO；扩展数据图1c）、内部损耗（二极管和导线）和反射回输入端口的功率。扩展数据图1d显示了在恒定的PO下，内部损耗与输入功率的关系。当PI增加时，内部损耗几乎按比例增加。由于内部损失的增加导致了热失控的风险，所以重要的是适当地调整PI。如扩展数据图1e所示，整流器（PO/PI）的最大效率约为67%，最优的输入功率为PO=500 mW时，这是连续飞行所需要的。这一效率可与较大的整流器49,50相媲美。

The d.c.–d.c. conversion efficiency

To determine the d.c.–d.c. conversion efficiency (Fig. 2c), the circuit was powered by a d.c. voltage source (E3631A, Agilent Technologies) and the input and output powers were evaluated by power analysers (PA1000, Tektronix). The input voltage was set to 3.7 V in this evaluation. The output voltage was set to 140 V. Using the fabricated high-voltage driver, we drive a pseudo-load with a duty ratio of 50%. Here we used an equivalent pseudo-load composed of resistor–capacitor parallel circuits (resistance, R = 133 kΩ; capacitance, C = 50 nF) instead of real actuators to prevent the breakage of the actuator caused by excessive drive power. The electrical characteristics of piezoelectric actuators can be modelled as a parallel circuit of a capacitor and a resistor–inductor–capacitor (RLC) series in a simplistic form51,52. The capacitance and resistance values are estimated based on the power consumption characteristics around the resonance frequency (the fitting method and result are shown in Extended Data Fig. 7). With a voltage amplitude of 140 V and a duty ratio of 50%, the power consumptions of the pseudo-load circuit and real actuator were 204 and 186 mW, respectively (the error was 8.6%). We adjusted the output power by changing the resistance and capacitance.
直流电-直流电转换效率。为了确定直流-直流转换效率（图2c），电路由一个直流电压源（E3631A，安捷伦科技公司）供电，输入和输出功率由功率分析仪（PA1000，泰克公司）评估。在这次评估中，输入电压被设定为3.7V。输出电压被设置为140V。使用制造的高压驱动器，我们以50%的占空比来驱动一个伪负载。这里我们使用了一个由电阻-电容并联电路组成的等效伪负载（电阻R = 133 kΩ；电容C = 50 nF）而不是真正的执行器，以防止因驱动力过大而造成执行器的断裂。压电执行器的电气特性可以被建模为一个电容和一个电阻-电感-电容（RLC）系列的并联电路，其形式很简单51,52。该电容和电阻值是根据谐振频率附近的功耗特性来估计的（拟合方法和结果见扩展数据图7）。在电压振幅为140 V和占空比为50%的情况下，伪负载电路和真实执行器的功耗分别为204和186 mW（误差为8.6%）。我们通过改变电阻和电容来调整输出功率。

Continuous driving test of circuit

In the continuous driving test (Fig. 2d), we used the equivalent pseudo-load employed in the measurement of the d.c.–d.c. converter. The RF wave was generated by a system composed of a programmable voltage source (2461, Keithley), 5.8 GHz RF amplifier (OSGM50-5.7G5.9G, Plasma Applications) and horn antenna (1424-15, Flann Microwave). The output RF power was 30 W. The output signal was recorded by an oscilloscope (DPO4034B, Tektronix).
电路的连续驱动测试。在连续驱动测试中（图2d），我们使用了测量直流-直流转换器时采用的等效伪负载。射频波是由一个可编程电压源（2461，吉时利）、5.8GHz射频放大器（OSGM50-5.7G5.9G，Plasma Applications）和喇叭天线（1424-15，Flann Microwave）组成的系统产生。输出的射频功率为30W。输出信号由示波器（DPO4034B，泰克公司）记录。

Actuator performance

The driving signal was generated by a combination of the high-voltage driver circuit and a d.c. voltage source (PMX350-0.2A, Kikusui Electronics). Here the input voltage was set to 140 V. The control signal for the high-voltage driver was generated by the microprocessor circuit, where the voltage and frequency of the control signal were set to 3.3 V and 115 Hz, respectively. The lift force was measured by a precision electrical balance (HR-100A, A&D Company); the experimental layout is the same as reported earlier18.
执行器的性能。驱动信号是由高压驱动电路和直流电压源（PMX350-0.2A，菊水电子）组合产生的。这里的输入电压被设定为140V。高压驱动器的控制信号由微处理器电路产生，其中控制信号的电压和频率分别设定为3.3V和115Hz。升降力由精密电子天平（HR-100A，A&D公司）测量；实验布局与之前的报告相同18.

Distance dependency of received power by dipole antenna

The RF wave was generated by the same instruments as used in the continuous driving test. The received power was measured by a combination of our fabricated FPC dipole antenna and an attenuator (−30 dB) (E9301B, Keysight Technologies) in series with a power sensor (E9304A or N1913A, Keysight Technologies).
偶极子天线的接收功率的距离依赖性。射频波是由用于连续飞行测试的相同仪器产生的。接收功率是由我们制造的FPC偶极子天线和一个与功率传感器（E9304A或N1913A，Keysight Technologies）串联的衰减器（-30dB） (E9301B, Keysight Technologies)组合测量的。

Flight demonstration

The RF wave was generated by the same instruments that were used in the continuous driving test. The output RF power was 30 W. The flight trajectory was captured by a motion-tracking system (Primex 41, OptiTrack) and its image was recorded by a colour camera included with the system. The vehicle was placed on a 160 mm square acrylic plate above the transmitting horn antenna with an initial distance of 70 mm from the transmitting antenna to the receiving dipole antenna.
飞行演示。射频波是由连续飞行试验中使用的相同仪器产生的。输出的射频功率为30W。飞行轨迹由运动跟踪系统（Primex 41，OptiTrack）捕获，其图像由该系统包括的彩色摄像机记录。飞行器被放置在发射角形天线上方的160毫米方形丙烯酸板上，从发射天线到接收偶极天线的初始距离为70毫米。
